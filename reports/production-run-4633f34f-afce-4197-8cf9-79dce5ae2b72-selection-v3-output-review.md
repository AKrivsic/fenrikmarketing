## Final Verdict

### 1. Did Creative Candidate Selection v3 produce noticeably stronger finished content?

**No — not noticeably.**

It produced a better selected concept and better opening frame than departure-board packages. The finished deliverable still fails mid-video and never demonstrates the product. A client would feel incremental, not transformative, change.

### 2. What improved the most?

Concept selection discipline: overturning high-creative / low-commercial absurd metaphors (boarding ticket / departure board). Opening hook + first-frame readability. LinkedIn/X copy quality.

### 3. What did not improve?

Mid-story abstract metaphors, product demonstration, spoken CTA, visual continuity, emotional human presence, Facebook coverage, and paid-ad readiness.

### 4. What is now the single biggest weakness of the entire pipeline?

**Storyboard/image generation does not honor the selected commercial concept through the middle** — it drifts into unreadable metaphor, then ends on a landing page instead of a working product demo.

### 5. If Sprint 4 starts tomorrow… what should be the ONLY priority?

**Force the selected commercial world to survive every beat:** no mid-video metaphor escape, and require a real product demonstration beat (chat answering the visitor’s question) with a spoken CTA that matches the package CTA.
