Opens on the gut-punch moment: a prospect discovering a dead social profile. The twist is that the silence wasn't laziness — it was a time problem with a real solution. The punchline reframes 'done-for-you' from a vague promise to a concrete output: finished video and captions across five platforms, delivered from nothing but a URL. Humor is self-aware and dry — the absurdity of losing a customer over something that's already been solved.
